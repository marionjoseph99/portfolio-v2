About

I'm a visual artist, 3D visualizer, and aspiring architect from General Trias, Cavite, Philippines, with a deep passion for art and design. My dedication to my craft is reflected in the effort I put into each project, ensuring that my clients are satisfied with the results. With extensive experience in 3D modeling using Revit, AutoCAD, Blender, and SketchUp, I create visually stunning designs that are both beautiful and functional. I also specialize in editing and enhancing my work using tools like Photoshop, Adobe Premiere, and Illustrator to deliver polished, professional results. Whether it's a small or large-scale project, I take pride in capturing the essence of what my clients envision.

Sumary

Marjo Paguia

An aspiring architect and visual artist with a strong foundation in design, rendering, and creative visualization, I am dedicated to transforming concepts into functional and aesthetically compelling spaces. Currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Architecture at National University PH - Laguna Campus, I am continuously refining my skills in spatial planning, sustainable design, and architectural visualization. My expertise extends across architectural renderings, digital art, and traditional illustrations, allowing me to merge technical precision with artistic expression. With proficiency in industry-standard software such as Revit, AutoCAD, SketchUp, Blender, and Adobe Creative Suite, I bring designs to life through detailed 3D models, realistic visualizations, and intricate artistic compositions. Beyond technical skills, I am passionate about exploring innovative design solutions that enhance both functionality and aesthetics. I believe that architecture is not just about structures but about creating meaningful experiences through thoughtful design. As I continue to grow in my field, I remain committed to pushing creative boundaries and delivering work that inspires and resonates with people.
