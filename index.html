About

I'm a visual artist, 3D visualizer, and aspiring architect from General Trias, Cavite, Philippines, with a deep passion for art and design. My dedication to my craft is reflected in the effort I put into each project, ensuring that my clients are satisfied with the results. With extensive experience in 3D modeling using Revit, AutoCAD, Blender, and SketchUp, I create visually stunning designs that are both beautiful and functional. I also specialize in editing and enhancing my work using tools like Photoshop, Adobe Premiere, and Illustrator to deliver polished, professional results. Whether it's a small or large-scale project, I take pride in capturing the essence of what my clients envision.
